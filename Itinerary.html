ITINERARY
DAY 1
Meet up with the boat and crew in Atlantis where you can enjoy a day at the slides and utilize the beautiful amenities in the resort.
DAY 2
A two hour ride south to Highbourne Cay provides an opportunity to go paddle boarding on the crystal clear water and a picnic on the beach.
DAY 3
Another two hour ride to Big Majors is where you will anchor for two nights where you can visit the famous Bahama pigs, deploy the sea pool, and enjoy water sports activities.
DAY 4
Cruise down to Staniel Cay where you can snorkel through the Thunderball Grotto made famous by the James Bond movie and investigate the underwater plane wreck with bounties of beautiful sea life.
DAY 5
An hour ride north to Warderick Wells Cay is where you will spend a night at anchor while enjoying the natural beauty of the Exuma Land and Sea Park. Snorkel, roam the sandbar, and explore the area by tender.
DAY 6
Explore the water around Norman's Cay. Once a safe haven for smugglers, this spot will provide a last night of fun in the water. Go tubing, wakeboarding, and explore the sandbars on the north side of the island.
DAY 7
Return to Atlantis for a final brunch.
