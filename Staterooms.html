STATEROOMS

Privée offers a luxurious interior that provides fantastic comfort and relaxation. With a full-beam master stateroom, VIP stateroom, and two twin staterooms, Privée can comfortably sleep up to eight guests. There is ample room to relax inside with the main salon below deck and an upper deck salon that features a retractable roof for fun under the sun in a comfortable indoor environment or a chance to gaze at the stars.
